Teacher Grading Questionnaire

01

Q: The teacher is generally well organized and prepared for class?

02

Q: The teacher maintains enough classroom discipline so the class and I can learn?

03

Q: Test and assignments are corrected and returned to me, and I know where I stand in this class in terms of my grade?

04

Q: he teacher uses a variety of activities (discussion, group work, lecture, labs, technology etc.) during class time?

05

Q: The teacher explains the material clearly and in ways that are easy to understand, offers alternative explainations or additional examples, and clear up confusion?

06

Q: My teacher made a positive impact on my learning experience?

07

Q: Any problems or misunderstandings were handled in a prompt, safe, supportive way?

08

Q: My teacher is available to talk outside of class if I need it?

09

Q: My teacher uses creative teaching methods?

10

Q: The grading methods my teacher uses seems fair for everyone?
